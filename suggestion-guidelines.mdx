Welcome! You have found our suggestions channel. Feel free to submit anything that your looking for a change in, or something you want added in the future. Please keep in mind the following:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pinging">
    Please do not ping any members of our team. We appriciate your suggestion and we need your patience. We **will** see it. No suggestion goes without review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Respect">
    Please respect all suggestion reviewers for their decision. If something is denied, please do not agrue with staff. You will be muted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Duplicate Suggestions">
    Please check and make sure the suggestion your about to make hasn't already been asked before. Duplicate suggestions will be deleted without warning.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  We appreciate everyone's suggestions in making our community greater.
</Note>
