If your looking to contact a support member, report someone, or ask a complex question, this is the place to do it! Please read the following guidelines to understand our rules for opening a ticket.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use of Support">
    Please only open a ticket if you need help with a complex request, are appealing something as a staff member. Reporting a member for violating our guidelines, or priavte suggestions. Do not open it for basic questions, please refer to general chat, and staff reports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Respect">
    Please respect all staff members within your ticket. Please stray away from profanity and rude language. We are here to help you. If you are unhappy with something, please kindly state your reason and we'll try to make it right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pinging Staff">
    Pinging members of our staff  team is prohibited. We appricate your patience. Our staff have lives outside of Discord. You may **only** ping us if you've been waiting for over 72 hours. Anytime before that and you will be moderated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Danger>
  Tickets are subject to close at any time for any reason with or without warning.
</Danger>
