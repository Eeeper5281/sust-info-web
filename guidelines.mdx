Welcome to SUST (Summer Update Search Team)! Our goal is to work together to discover, document, and discuss ERLC Summer Update conten**t. Please follow these rules to keep the server organized and enjoyable.**

<Warning>
  All rules are subject to change at any time **without** warning!
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discord's Terms of Use and Community Guidelines">
    Discord's Terms of Use must be followed at **all** times within our community. Failure to follow this will result in an instant ban without appeal. Some examples of content that violates the Terms of Use include:

    - NSFW or Not Safe For Work Content
    - Sexual Content or Messages
    - Extreme Gore or Violence
    - Extreme Profanity, Racial Profiling, and Hate Speech
    - Targeting minority groups; this includes Race, Gender Identity, Sexuality, Religion, and more.
    - [View the Full Guidelines Here](https://discord.com/terms)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Respect and Profanity">
    Treat all members with respect. No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks. Healthy debates are fine; arguments and toxicity are not. Mild profanity is permitted. Please keep it minimal. Hate speech against other members is against our guidelines. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Channels">
    Keep discussions related to ERLC, its updates, leaks, theories, and findings in the appropriate channels. Use off-topic channels for unrelated conversations. Post discoveries in the appropriate channels. Keep reports organized so the team can easily review them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Falsifying Information">
    Don't spread fake leaks or intentionally misleading information. If you're sharing speculation, clearly label it as a theory. Whenever possible, provide screenshots, videos, or other evidence. All leaks must show the source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Spamming">
    Spamming within our community is prohibited. Please do not send large chunks of messages with the intent of annoyance or causing a scene.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stealing">
    Credit others for their discoveries. Share information to help the community instead of competing against each other. Don't 'steal' findings from others.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Impersonation">
    Impersonation of any kind is strictly prohibited. Please make sure your identity and profile is who you really are. You may not impersonate people such as:

    - Staff Members
    - PRC Developers
    - PRC Moderators
    - Other Community Members
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Danger>
  Follow all staff instructions when given. Not every rule is explicitly listed. Staff members have the final say.
</Danger>
