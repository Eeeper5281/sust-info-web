---
title: "Emergency Response Liberty County Guidelines"
---

Hiya! Welcome to our game! We're glad that your interested in playing. We require that all members fully read over our guidelines for smooth and realistic roleplays. By joining, you agree that you have read this fully.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Roblox's Terms of Use">
    Roblox's Terms of Use must be strictly be followed at all times. This includes chat restrictions, avatars, and roleplays that happen within our server.

    <Tip>
      [Read the full guidelines here.](https://en.help.roblox.com/hc/en-us/articles/115004647846-Roblox-Terms-of-Use)
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Realisim">
    We focus on realism within our server. This means that your are within roleplay at all times. There shall never be a time where someone is 'out of roleplay'. The only exception is for moderation scenes. All actions you commit must be realistic and replicable in real life. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Death Match">
    You must not kill, punch, or harm anybody unless your are within a reasonable roleplay. You may not ram or damage someone's car without being engaged in roleplay. Please act realistically with weapons and vehicles. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Department Guidelines">
    While roleplaying as a department member, you must follow these rules, as well as the other rules within this document.

    - Ensure your using the provided uniforms
    - Only use liveries that are not labeled "WL"
    - Do not commit any crimes
    - Remain realistic at all times
  </Step>
  <Step title="Staff">
    Our staff is provided by us to ensure that our roleplays remain smooth and realistic. Whenever a staff member approaches you, do not run away or leave the game. Please stay and listen to all instructions. If you are driving, please pull over.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restricted Roleplays">
    The following roleplays are set by our ownership team, and are not allowed in any form. Participating in roleplays like this will result in punishment.

    - Drug Roleplay (Intoxication Allowed)
    - Suicide or Self-Harm Roleplay
    - Terroristic / Bomb Roleplay
    - Sexual Roleplay of **any kind**
    - Hate against a specific group

    In addition to this, roleplays must not happen within set safezones. The following are listed below.

    - City and Springfield Spawns
    - Police Department
    - Sheriff's Office
    - Both Fire Departments
    - Department of Transportation
    - Hospital
    - Both Gun S
  </Step>
</Steps>
