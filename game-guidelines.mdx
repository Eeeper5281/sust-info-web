Hiya! Welcome to our game! We're glad that your interested in playing. We require that all members fully read over our guidelines for smooth and realistic roleplays. By joining, you agree that you have read this fully.

<Warning>
  All rules are subject to change at any time **without** warning!
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Roblox's Terms of Use">
    Roblox's Terms of Use must be strictly be followed at all times. This includes chat restrictions, avatars, and roleplays that happen within our server.

    <Tip>
      [Read Roblox's Full Guidelines Here](https://en.help.roblox.com/hc/en-us/articles/115004647846-Roblox-Terms-of-Use)
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Realisim">
    We focus on realism within our server. This means that your are within roleplay at all times. There shall never be a time where someone is 'out of roleplay'. The only exception is for moderation scenes. All actions you commit must be realistic and replicable in real life. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Death Match">
    You must not kill, punch, or harm anybody unless your are within a reasonable roleplay. You may not ram or damage someone's car without being engaged in roleplay. Please act realistically with weapons and vehicles. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Department Guidelines">
    While roleplaying as a department member, you must follow these rules, as well as the other rules within this document.

    - Ensure you're using the provided uniforms
    - Only use liveries that are not labeled "WL"
    - Do not commit any crimes
    - Remain realistic at all times
    - Blocky avatars are **required**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Staff">
    Our staff is provided by us to ensure that our roleplays remain smooth and realistic. Whenever a staff member approaches you, do not run away or leave the game. Please stay and listen to all instructions. If you are driving, please pull over.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restricted Roleplays">
    The following roleplays are set by our ownership team, and are not allowed in any form. Participating in roleplays like this will result in punishment.

    - Drug Roleplay (Intoxication Allowed)
    - Suicide or Self-Harm Roleplay
    - Terroristic / Bomb Roleplay
    - Sexual Roleplay of **any kind**
    - Hate against a specific group

    In addition to this, roleplays must not happen within set safezones. The following are listed below.

    - City and Springfield Spawns
    - Police Department
    - Sheriff's Office
    - Both Fire Departments
    - Department of Transportation
    - Hospital
    - Both Gun Stores
    - Court House

    <Note>
      Shooting, harming others, or committing crimes within safe zones is not allowed. Minor roleplays may still happen.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Vehicles">
    While driving a car, you must remain realistic. You must not exceed 125mph\+ without roleplay reason. (Ex. Pursuit). All crashes must be roleplayed, unless **both** members agree that it should be voided or it is under the speed of 30mph. 

    The following cars are restricted to boosters or VIP members. You may boost our server within our Discord. VIP is handpicked. Please do not ask for it.

    The following cars are fully prohibited and are only accessible to server staff members. Consider applying; we're always looking for people to join our team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Avatar's">
    All avatars must be realistic at all times, and be compliant with Roblox's Terms of Use. Accessories must be connected to your body, and not floating. The following is considered unrealistic. 

    - Blocky Avatars Required for** Department Roleplays**
    - No floating accessories or gear
    - Skin tone must be a naturally occurring color for the human body
    - Sexually suggestive avatars
    - Large avatars
    - Tiny avatars
    - Animals

    <Danger>
      Staff members always have the final call on whether an avatar is realistic or not.
    </Danger>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gun Regulations">
    While using a weapon, you must follow strict guidelines. The following weapons are restricted, and not usable by anybody other then VIP and Server Staff.

    - LMT
    - AK47
    - Remington MSR
    - M249
    - Orsis T 5000

    While having a weapon, if you have something other then a pistol, gun motion is required in Roblox Chat at all times before taking it out. You musn't pull a large weapon out of nowhere without a visible strap on your avatar. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unwritten Rules">
    Not every enforceable rule is written, and may be adjusted at any time. You are not subject to notice for any changes. However, major changes will be updated via our Discord. Staff members always have the final say for a punishment. Claiming that something is not written within our rules will **not** be taken as a defence. Please use common sense while roleplaying.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discord Membership">
    We require that all members are within our discord server prior to joining. Staff members and automated bots will be enforcing this. Please contact server staff if your are unable to join. In-order for our staff to stop contacting you, verification must be completed. You may do so [here](https://discord.com/channels/1524808837687676949/1529684191963254835).
  </Step>
</Steps>
